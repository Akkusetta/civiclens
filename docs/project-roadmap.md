# CivicLens Project Roadmap

## Project Vision

CivicLens is an AI-powered community intelligence platform that enables citizens to share civic issues, local experiences, safety concerns, and community insights tied to real-world locations. The platform uses AI to transform individual reports into actionable community intelligence.
